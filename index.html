Design Principles

THE PHILOSOPHY

"S.E engines are weather vanes — they point in the direction of the macro wind. T.E engines are waterwheels — they extract energy from flow regardless of direction. O.E engines are memory — they watch everything and build the dataset that will eventually power autonomous cross-engine intelligence."

— CCE Platform Philosophy

01
No Prediction
The system reacts to confirmed signals. It never forecasts. A weather vane is always correct. A weather forecast is often wrong.
02
No Emotion
State transitions follow strict mathematical logic. Every decision is deterministic and auditable. Fear and greed are inputs, not drivers.
03
Environment First
Capital deploys only when the environment — described by the combination of signals — reaches a state that historically precedes the target move.
04
Full Transparency
Every signal, state, trade, and metric is logged to SQLite. Nothing happens without a record. The system is always auditable.
